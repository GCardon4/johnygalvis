Johny Galvis is a contemporary photographer whose work bridges the gap between raw documentarian instinct and highly structured editorial design. Based between New York and Paris, his approach treats negative space as a primary subject.

With a background in architectural design, every frame is meticulously composed to eliminate visual noise, allowing the core narrative—whether in fashion, portraiture, or conceptual landscapes—to resonate with absolute clarity.
